Myths and Facts

Myth : Manual therapy documentation is more reliable than digital systems.

Fact: Digital documentation reduces errors, ensures real-time updates, and improves collaboration between doctors, making the process more efficient and reliable.

Myth : Junior doctors don’t need real-time feedback; it can wait until scheduled reviews.

Fact: Immediate feedback from senior doctors helps junior doctors improve their skills faster and enhances patient outcomes through timely adjustments in care.

Myth : Patients need to visit the hospital in person for every consultation or therapy session.

Fact: With our platform, patients can access digital consultations, manage their treatment plans online, and participate in remote therapy activities, reducing the need for frequent hospital visits.

Myth : Automating patient allocation may cause errors and mismatches in care assignments.

Fact: Automated patient allocation ensures that cases are assigned based on availability and expertise, minimizing errors and ensuring the best possible care.

Myth : Therapies can’t be effectively monitored or assessed without in-person evaluations.

Fact: Digital tracking systems allow for continuous monitoring of therapy sessions and patient progress, with real-time data accessible to both junior and senior doctors for accurate assessments.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact my doctor directly?

You can directly message your doctor by entering their unique doctor code in the prescription form on the SoundSteps portal. Use the query section to send them messages or updates.

What remote activities can I do at home to improve my speech?

Access our open-source activities and interactive speech therapy games available for free in the SoundSteps portal, designed to enhance your therapy from home.

How do I know if my doctor has reviewed my prescription?

Once your prescription is reviewed and approved by your senior doctor, you will receive a notification in your SoundSteps account with their feedback and suggestions.

What should I do if I miss a therapy session?

If you miss a session, the system will notify both you and your therapist. You can easily reschedule the session through the booking feature in the portal.

How are junior and senior doctors involved in my treatment?

Junior doctors manage your day-to-day treatment and prescriptions, while senior doctors supervise, review, and provide approval for your therapy plan, ensuring quality care at all times.
